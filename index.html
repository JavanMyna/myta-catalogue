  • Project 4flat Google Sheets study tracker A Google Sheets study tracker, built with a study partner during Matriculation Sem 2 — logging every practice question and quietly running statistics on our own weak spots.

    A Google Sheets tracker built from scratch with a study partner during my final semester of Matriculation. Not a school assignment — just two people who wanted an actual system for revising, instead of a textbook vaguely marked ‘done.’

    Every practice question either of us attempted got logged: source paper, subject, marks, time spent. The sheet did the boring-but-useful part automatically — accumulated marks, mean, and standard deviation per question — so a bad score would show up as either ‘just an off day’ or ‘an actual weak spot,’ instead of both looking identical.

    There’s also a shared log — dates, subjects, and study session blocks running from March through May, right through exam season. Not glamorous, mostly timestamps and question numbers, but it’s the clearest proof I have that this wasn’t theoretical. We used it, every week, until the semester ended.

    Semester 2 CGPA: 4.00
